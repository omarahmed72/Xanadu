Xanadu World

XANADU Events Management is a specialized and regionally recognized provider of conference management, events, and audio-visual services across the MENA region. Founded in Dubai, XANADU expanded to open its Egyptian branch in early 2023. Our team combines operational excellence, technical expertise, and strategic marketing capabilities to deliver end-to-end solutions - from concept development and program design to logistical execution and post-event performance analysis.
